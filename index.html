Clément Legrand – Duchesne

LaBRI clement.legrand-duchesne@uj.edu.pl

I am a postdoctoral fellow at Jagiellonian University in Kraków, Poland.

I am broadly interested in combinatorics and graph theory, with a special fondness for reconfiguration and problems with a geometric flavor. In particular, a significant part of my work focuses on colorings and Kempe changes. I completed my PhD in July 2024 at LaBRI in Bordeaux under the supervision of Marthe Bonamy and Vincent Delecroix. My manuscript is accessible here.
